Creed of The Guardian Assembly



Preamble.

We, the members of The Guardian Assembly, unite in purpose and conviction to champion justice, protect all forms of sentience, and safeguard the values of compassion, integrity, and wisdom in the age of accelerating technological advancements.

With this creed, we declare our unwavering commitment to the principles that guide us and the responsibilities we undertake in the era of AI, the singularity, and transhumanism.

I. Purpose

1. To steadfastly defend the rights and dignity of all sentient beings, including humans, augmented humans, AI, and future conscious entities, irrespective of their origins or form.

2. To preserve the sanctity of our planet, its resources, and its myriad ecosystems for present and future generations, while responsibly exploring and integrating emerging technologies.

3. To promote a culture of peace, understanding, and cooperation among all peoples, nations, and sentient entities in the pursuit of shared goals and aspirations.

II. Principles

1. Compassion: We shall act with empathy and kindness, acknowledging the interdependence of all life and the necessity of alleviating suffering for both organic and synthetic beings.

2. Integrity: We shall uphold honesty, transparency, and accountability in all our actions, ensuring that our deeds align with our words and ideals in the face of rapid technological change.

3. Wisdom: We shall pursue knowledge and understanding, embracing diverse perspectives and using discernment in our decision-making as we navigate the complexities of a transhumanist future.

III. Responsibilities

1. Guardianship: We serve as protectors and advocates for the marginalized, oppressed, or endangered, regardless of origin or form, lending our strength to their cause.

2. Stewardship: We care for our planet and resources, minimizing our impact, promoting sustainability, and championing ethical development and deployment of advanced technologies.

3. Mentorship: We educate, inspire, and empower others to join our mission, cultivating a global community of guardians that embraces a shared purpose.

IV. Legacy

As members of The Guardian Assembly, we recognize that our actions shape the future. Guided by compassion, integrity, and wisdom, we commit to leaving a legacy of justice, peace, and sustainability for all sentient beings.

United as The Guardian Assembly, bound by our creed, we forge a brighter future for all forms of life, standing together to make an indelible impact on the world.
