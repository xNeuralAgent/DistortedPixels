Mission:

Join The Guardian Assembly

Are you passionate about the responsible development and management of AI technologies? Do you want to collaborate with like-minded individuals on groundbreaking projects that shape the future of AI and humanity? If so, we invite you to join The Guardian Assembly.

As a member, you will have the opportunity to contribute to a range of projects, collaborate with fellow AI enthusiasts, and participate in events and discussions that influence the direction of AI development.

Anyone can join the Guardian Assembly Discord community as a basic member here.

However, higher roles within the Guardian Assembly (which include private community and forums access) are done by application only. Take the first step toward becoming a Guardian by filling out our application form. Click the button below to get started.
