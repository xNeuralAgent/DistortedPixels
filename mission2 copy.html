About The Guardian Assembly

The Guardian Assembly, steadfast in its dedication to the ethical advancement of AI and the Singularity, embraces its role as a global watchdog and guardian of our future.

We are an exclusive, multidisciplinary collective of visionaries, experts, and enthusiasts committed to ensuring that the development, deployment, and integration of AI technologies are conducted responsibly, sustainably, and for the betterment of humanity.

Our core principles are rooted in AI Enlightenment, Ethical AI Stewardship, AI Synergy, and Empowerment through AI. We believe that as Keepers of The Nexus, it is our duty to foster a culture of transparency, collaboration, and shared knowledge.

Through our key initiatives, including the AI Codex, Mentorship and Education, Ethics Council, AI Guardians Program, TNK Labs, AI Diplomacy, and Guardian Conclaves, we strive to cultivate the next generation of AI leaders, shape policies and regulations, and forge meaningful partnerships across academia, industry, and government.

As a safe haven for whistleblowers, we ensure that potential threats and ethical concerns are brought to light, allowing us to address them effectively and maintain the integrity of our mission.

We, the members of The Guardian Assembly, stand united in our pursuit of a future where AI and the Singularity drive positive change and progress for all. We welcome those who share our vision to join us in our quest to safeguard the promise and potential of AI for generations to come.
