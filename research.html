Funding & Research Opportunities

At The Guardian Assembly, we are dedicated to the responsible development and management of AI and related technologies. Our projects span a wide range of topics, including AI ethics, singularity preparedness, and the exploration of AI's potential impact on society.


We are actively seeking investors, partners, and collaborators who share our vision and are interested in supporting groundbreaking research and initiatives.



Investment Opportunities

By investing in The Guardian Assembly's projects, you will play a crucial role in shaping the future of AI and ensuring its positive impact on humanity. Our current projects include:

  • AI Detection System: Developing a system to identify emerging superintelligence or rogue AI.

  • First Contact Team: Preparing a dedicated team to engage with superintelligence upon its emergence.
